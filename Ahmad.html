About Me

I hold a BS (Hons) in Statistics with distinction from GC University Lahore and an MPhil in Statistics where I graduated as a Gold Medalist.

My professional journey includes serving as a Visiting Lecturer at GC University Lahore, working as a Scientific Officer (Statistics) at Ayub Agricultural Research Institute, and currently contributing as a Statistical Officer at the Punjab Bureau of Statistics.

I specialize in industrial data analysis, experimental design, and comprehensive reporting. My approach combines academic rigor with practical insights to deliver solutions that bridge statistical theory with real-world applications.
